MISSION AND VISION
Our methods, values, goals, and achievements

Our vision

Leading producer of livestock and other agricultural products

OUR MISSION

  • Produce and sell high quality poultry products, snails, mushroom, other livestock.
  • To meet the expectation and demands of our customers.
  • Training more youth in modernize agricultural practices as a means of job creation and sustain it.

WHAT WE DO

We offer practical training for the youth in sustainable agriculture in order to intentionally create the zeal for farming in the youth and to provide various services needed

OUR GOALS

  1. Satisfy human food and clothing (cotton, wool, leather) needs
  2. Enhance environmental quality and natural resources
  3. Use non-renewable resources more efficiently
  4. Take better advantage of on-farm resources
  5. Employ natural and biological controls for pests and disease
  6. Intentionally sustain the economic viability of farming
  7. Enhance the quality of life of farmers and society as a whole
