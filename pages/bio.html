JOSEPH OCLOO FARMS (JO Farms)


Joseph Ocloo Farms is a sole proprietorship formed in the year, 2001. It was located at Segbedeme a suburb of Hohoe in the Hohoe Municipality but now currently situated at Hohoe, Godenu Dornormade, 5km off the Eastern Corridor Road. The enterprise engaged in the production of livestock, crops, and produces poultry and products. The enterprise however, sells mushroom and farm produce each production cycle.


Joseph Ocloo Farms is a registered farming company that is owned and managed by Rev. Joseph Kwabena Ocloo assisted by a Production Manager and Field workers. The company will be partly financed by the owner and a Venture Capital


It is in pursuance of the objective "achieving self-sufficiency in terms of food production", that Ocloo farms Limited, a privately owned company has entered into the production of crops and livestock. One of the many activities of the Company is poultry production to produce quality poultry products to feed the Ghanaian population and also to generate income through the employment of the youth in the catchment area.
