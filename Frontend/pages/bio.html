Institutional Profile

Joseph Ocloo Farms

A private agricultural enterprise founded in 2001 with a clear mandate to combine productive farming and skills development.

Founder and Leadership

Rev. Joseph Kwabena Ocloo serves as Founder and Chief Executive Officer, leading operational strategy, institutional partnerships, and youth-focused training initiatives.

Under his leadership, JO Farms has expanded production systems while maintaining strong social and environmental commitments.

Who We Are

JO Farms operates in Hohoe, Godenu Dornormade, approximately 5km from the Eastern Corridor Road. The enterprise focuses on livestock and crop production, product supply, and practical agribusiness training.

What Makes Us Official

  • Structured farm divisions with dedicated managers and field teams
  • Recognizable operational base and on-site infrastructure
  • Documented training workflows for youth and institutions
  • Long-term commitment to food system development

Our Vision

To be a leading agricultural production and training institution in Ghana, recognized for innovation, quality, and community impact.

Our Mission

  • Produce and deliver high-quality farm products for domestic and regional markets.
  • Train youth and farmer groups in practical, modern, and sustainable farm systems.
  • Create employment opportunities through enterprise-driven agriculture.
  • Promote responsible stewardship of natural resources.

Strategic Priorities

  1. Strengthen farm productivity through improved practices and mechanization.
  2. Expand value addition and processing opportunities for key products.
  3. Build market access through structured supply partnerships.
  4. Develop talent pipelines through continuous training and mentorship.
  5. Support climate-smart farming and afforestation interventions.

Core Operating Values

Integrity

Transparent operations, credible reporting, and accountable leadership.

Quality

High standards across production, handling, and customer service delivery.

Impact

Practical interventions that improve livelihoods and food security outcomes.

Operational Scope

Livestock Systems

Poultry and related livestock systems supporting regular supply and workforce training.

Crop and Fungi

Commercial mushroom and crop lines designed for food quality and market continuity.

Specialty Farming

Snail and specialty lines offering diversification and high-value market opportunities.
